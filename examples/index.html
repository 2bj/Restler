Examples

Luracast Restler is a microframework to expose php methods as RESTful API. Best way to learn is by examples, here you can find the examples that shows every feature of Restler and how to use them.

We have created it in such a way that they can be tested on your local host.

You can check the source code, try some of the API's exposed at the comfort of your browser
